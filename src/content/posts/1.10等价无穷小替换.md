# $x\rightarrow 0$时
## 三角族
$$
\begin{align}
\sin x  & \sim  x  \\
\tan x  & \sim  x  \\
\arcsin x  & \sim x  \\
\arctan x  & \sim x  \\
\end{align}
$$
![File-等价无穷小公式-2520251215](/images/from-obsidian/File-等价无穷小公式-2520251215.png)
## 幂族
$$
\begin{align} 
1 - \cos x \sim \frac{1}{2}x^2  \\
\cos x - 1 \sim -\frac{1}{2}x^2  \\

\end{align} 

$$
## 根号族
$$
\begin{align} 
\sqrt[n]{ 1 + x } - 1 \sim \frac{1}{n}x  \\
\end{align} 
$$
## $\ln$、$e^x$族
$$
\begin{align}
\ln_{}(1+x) \sim x \\
e^x - 1 \sim x \\
\end{align}
$$
![File-等价无穷小公式-2520251215-1](/images/from-obsidian/File-等价无穷小公式-2520251215-1.png)
$$
\begin{align}
a^x - 1 \sim x \ln a  \\
\end{align}
$$
