#### 微分定义
$\text{若 } y = f(x)，\text{则 } dy = f'(x)dx$
微分$dy$是增量的线性主部
#### 微分公式
$$
\begin{align*}
&d(x^n) = nx^{n-1}dx \\
&d(e^x) = e^x dx \\
&d(\ln|x|) = \frac{1}{x} dx \\
&d(\sin x) = \cos x dx \\
&d(\cos x) = -\sin x dx
\end{align*}
$$
